RADWIMPS

The band was formed in 2001 by Yojiro Noda and Akira Kuwahara while they were still attending high school. They began to gain recognition after releasing their first EP in 2003. They released their first full length album in 2005, titled “Radwimps”, and it was an instant success in Japan. In 2006, they released their second album “Atarashii Sekai”, which also achieved commercial success. Style of Music RADWIMPS’s sound is a unique blend of pop and rock music. They have been praised for their ability to create catchy melodies that are balanced out by heavier rock-influenced instrumentals. They often write lyrics that are both deeply personal and abstract at the same time. Notable Songs Some of RADWIMPS’s most popular songs include “Order Made”, “Dream Lantern”, “Nandemonaiya”, and “Sparkle”.
