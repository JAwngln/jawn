KDA

The name "K/DA" is an in-game term in League of Legends that refers to a player's kills, deaths, and assists. The creation of K/DA was based in part on the desire of Riot Games, League's developers to "see their company more as a full-fledged music label in the future". Although the initial pitch to create K/DA came sometime in 2017, according to Tantula, the idea behind it "had been brewing for about five years.
